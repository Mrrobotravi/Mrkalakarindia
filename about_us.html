about me

Rahul Kumawat, a.k.a. Mr Kalakaar, is an multidisciplinary artist and interior designer from Gujarat, India. He was selected as one of the top 100 record holders on World Stage-2022 by the World Records Union. He attended a tea party with the Prime Minister of India, the DG NCC, and the Chiefs of the Army, Navy, and Air Staff. He created the world's largest chalk drawing in April 2022. As Asia's first astronaut, Pham Tuan, Honoured him in September 2022. His name appears in two "India book of Records". His World Record Artwork was displayed in World's Largest NFT Event "NFT NYC 2023" at New York City.

He dropped out of the bachelor of commerce program to pursue his art career. To explore art mediums, he works in a stationery store. Using a variety of mediums, he challenges himself to create great artwork. Each artwork carries a message and a story. He represented self-dependent India at Nationals with marble chips of a unique medium. He has accomplished more than 120 national and international art projects in realistic pencil sketches, canvas painting, stippling, scribing, iron nails, mosaic art, and others.
